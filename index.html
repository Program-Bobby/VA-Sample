Why Use TafiPal

Your number one trip planning and reminder app. TafiPal is your best friend when it comes to organizing your travel. Your go to app for smart planning of smooth and safe trips and tours.

Location Map-View

Awoken pre-trip butterflies in your tummy!! Enjoy a map view virtual experience of the beautiful places you intend to visit at your destination even before the trip starts.

Checklist Reminders

Okay!! You remember how you forgot your yellow purse and power bank on your trip to Dubai? Now get reminders of your Checklist so you never forget essential items and task for your trip.

Trip-Mates Notifications

Like to share your experience with your loved ones : trip details, flight status and safety alerts ? Invite virtual or physical trip-mates to your travel experience.

Flight Status-Updates

What if you have canceled and delayed flights? Annoying stuff right? Receive notification on your flight departure time and share status-update with your most preferred trip-mates.

Plane Sight (TafiPal Pro)

Airplane mode flight monitoring, just so you can track your departure and arrival time even in airplane mode.

Safe Drive (TafiPal Pro)

Want an extra layer of security while you are on your trip? Auto-alarm raiser for detected reckless driving. Also, allows you to raise alarm for road hazards like robbery, accidents.
